## 📖 About ClaudeBar

**_ClaudeBar_** is a native desktop indicator that surfaces your **_Claude.ai_** _Max-plan_ session and weekly usage as two color-coded bars — directly in your menu bar, system tray, or panel. It signs you in with _OAuth_ (PKCE + loopback) and stores your token in the OS-native secret store: the _macOS_ Keychain, _Windows_ DPAPI, or the _FreeDesktop_ Secret Service on _Linux_. A consistent feature set supports _macOS_, _Windows_, and every major _Linux_ desktop environment — _GNOME_, _KDE_ Plasma, _Cinnamon_, _XFCE_, _MATE_, _Budgie_, and _LXQt_. Usage thresholds, poll interval, and display options are all configurable, and the app speaks to nothing beyond `api.anthropic.com` and `console.anthropic.com` on your behalf.
